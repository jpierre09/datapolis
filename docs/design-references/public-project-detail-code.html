Apariciones ovnis en el mundial

An editorial investigation into the startling correlation between football world cup matches and unidentified flying object sightings across the globe.

Published

Nov 2024

"¿Las masas de personas atraen seres de otros planetas?"

The Narrative

For decades, eyewitness reports of mysterious lights in the sky have spiked during major global events. However, none show as consistent a correlation as the FIFA World Cup. Our team analyzed over 50 years of sighting data, mapping coordinates against stadium locations and kick-off times.

The results suggest that these phenomena are not random. Whether it is the concentrated human emotion, the massive power consumption of modern stadiums, or something more inexplicable, the data points to a recurring presence that monitors our most collective celebrations.
